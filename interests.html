Interests

Often the case what makes a person interesting is in the hobbies they do outside their work. Whether it be extraordinary things like parachuting or hang gliding or to simply reading. It is the often the interests that catches a person's attention.

Sports:

Sports have always been an endearing passion of mine ever since I was a little kid. Never constrained to one discipline of sport I moved around a lot trying almost everything when I was young whether that be rowing along the Lee or practicing tennis in the courts of Douglas. However, after moving into first year of secondary school I figured I ought to stay and achieve mastery or at least some level of competency in a few sports. The two sports I had chosen were the ones that held my interest for the longest time being archery and taekwondo. I was lucky to have chosen these as my sports because as I soon found out, I often did not have enough time to dedicate myself to these sports especially around the exam years of third year and sixth year. However now that I am in university, I have now found time for these two sports again, catching up on lost time. Despite the setbacks I will continue to pursue these sports throughout my university years and hopefully into the future.
